Advancing Black Geoscientist Professionals and Businesses.

Black Geoscientists Forum is an association of black geoscience professionals aimed at aiding, empowering, and developing black geoscientists. It is a flagship organization with a mandate to aid in career development, company/professional networking, and access to markets just to name a few.

The Black Geoscientist Forum captures, develops, and applies cutting-edge thinking from within and beyond the South African geosciences to create ideas with actual effect. We engage in innovative ideas that influence the present and future of the field of Geosciences.
